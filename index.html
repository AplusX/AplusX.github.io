Short Bio

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). My research interest mainly focuses on the intersection of computer vision and computer graphics, including but not limited to interactive VR/AR/MR rendering, HDR lighting estimation and panoramic scene understanding. My reseach goal is to enable photo-realistic content creation automatically for VR/AR/MR applications. Recently, I am also interested in perceptually realistic graphics and VR/AR/MR medical simulation training and education.
